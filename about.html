Frequently Asked Questions

What is MovieBot?
MovieBot is a Telegram bot that lets you search and download 50,000+ movies instantly. Just type a movie name in the bot and get the file delivered to your chat in seconds.
Is it free to use?
Yes! You get 1 free movie download per day. For unlimited downloads, you can upgrade to our affordable premium plans starting from just ₹9 for a 3-day trial.
How do I start using the bot?
Simple! Open Telegram, search for @extractkrlo_bot, press Start, join our channel @movies_updates00, and then search any movie name. That's it!
What quality are the movies?
Movies are available in 480p, 720p, 1080p and even 4K depending on the plan. Free users get 480p, Gold users get 1080p HD, and Royal users get 4K quality.
What is auto-delete?
To keep our service running, downloaded files are automatically deleted after a set time. Free = 2 min, Gold = 15 min, Royal = no auto-delete. Forward files to your Saved Messages immediately!
What are the premium plans?
We offer Trial (₹9/3 days), Mini (₹49/15 days), Gold (₹79/30 days), Diamond (₹349/90 days), and Royal (₹599/year). All premium plans include unlimited downloads.
How do I pay for premium?
Type /plans in the bot, select a plan, and pay via UPI. After payment, share the screenshot in the bot and our admin will verify and activate your plan within minutes.
Can I get a free trial?
Yes! When you hit your daily limit, click the "🎁 Activate 3 Days FREE" button to get a free trial with unlimited downloads. No payment needed for the first time!
How do I contact support?
Type /contact in the bot to create a support ticket. Our admin will reply within 24 hours. All conversations are anonymous for your privacy.
Can I earn free premium?
Yes! Use /referrals to get your invite link. Every 5 friends you invite earn you free premium days. You can also earn points via /spin (daily lucky spin) and /streak (daily login rewards).
